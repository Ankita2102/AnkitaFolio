Medical Billing Reimagined: A UI/UX Case Study

  • Beatriz Garcia de Prado
  • UI/UX Case Study
  • 14

All Data Health, a company that collects medical billing information from physicians and sends it to insurers for payment, hired me to redesign its billing experience in late 2020. At the time, its physician clients were accustomed to filling out paper superbillsâ€”comprehensive forms that include diagnoses, procedures, and insurance claim codes.

To increase the speed and accuracy of its claims process, company leaders wanted to entice physicians to submit online superbills (called e-superbills) through an existing physician portal, but the physicians resisted. They continued mailing paper forms to All Data Health, where billing specialists entered the data manually before submitting claims to insurers.

Doctors are notoriously wary of new technology. Security, productivity, and affordability concerns cause physicians to cling to familiar methods and tools. My mission was to reimagine the existing medical billing software and make it so user-friendly that physicians would happily abandon their paper superbills.
