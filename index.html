About me

Welcome to my portfolio website! I'm Ankita Gupta, a passionate and creative UI/UX designer with a keen eye for detail and a love for crafting delightful user experiences. With 4.9years of industry experience, I have had the opportunity to work on a variety of exciting projects across different domains. My design approach revolves around putting the user at the center of everything I create. I believe that intuitive and visually appealing interfaces can make a significant impact on how users interact with digital products. Whether it's a mobile app, a website, or a software interface, I strive to make each interaction seamless, engaging, and memorable.

I have a strong foundation in user research, wireframing, prototyping, and visual design. I enjoy diving deep into user insights, conducting usability tests, and gathering feedback to inform my design decisions. By empathizing with the end-users, I can understand their needs, preferences, and pain points, allowing me to create solutions that truly resonate with them. My expertise lies in creating user-centric designs using industry-standard tools such as HTML,CSS,Bootstrap Figma and Adobe Photoshop. I am well-versed in designing responsive interfaces that provide a consistent experience across different devices and screen sizes. Additionally, I keep up with the latest design trends, ensuring that my work is always fresh, modern, and in line with industry standards.

Throughout my career, I have collaborated closely with cross-functional teams, including developers, product managers, and stakeholders, to ensure smooth project execution and successful delivery. I thrive in collaborative environments, where I can share ideas, receive constructive feedback, and iterate on designs to achieve the best possible outcomes. Beyond my technical skills, I bring a strong passion for problem-solving and a drive for continuous learning. I am always seeking opportunities to enhance my knowledge, whether it's through attending design conferences, participating in design challenges, or exploring emerging technologies.

In my portfolio, you will find a collection of my notable projects, showcasing my design process, problem-solving abilities, and the impact of my work on user experience. I hope you enjoy exploring my designs and get a sense of my commitment to creating thoughtful, user-centered solutions. Thank you for visiting my portfolio website, and I look forward to the possibility of collaborating with you to create exceptional digital experiences.
